FAQ

Common questions.

What NFC cards do I need to buy? +
Any standard NFC card works — NTAG213, NTAG215, or NTAG216. A pack of 50 costs around $10–15 on Amazon. No proprietary hardware, no vendor lock-in.
Do my members need to download an app? +
No. Members get a physical card — that's it. Only you as the gym owner need to install Taply. Members tap or scan and walk in.
What if my phone doesn't support NFC? +
There's a QR code fallback built in that works on every phone with a camera. NFC is the main experience but you're never blocked without it.
Can I freeze a membership without deleting it? +
Yes. Freeze any membership with one tap. The expiry timer pauses and resumes when you unfreeze. Perfect for members who travel or take breaks.
Is there a limit on members? +
No limit. $8/month covers unlimited members, unlimited check-ins, and all features regardless of your gym size.
